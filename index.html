About GeoWav

Smart Location Automation for Everyday Life

GeoWav is a mobile application that combines real-time geofencing with automated WhatsApp messaging. Define locations that matter to you, link your contacts, and let the app handle notifications when you arrive or leave — automatically and reliably.

Beyond messaging, GeoWav supports location-based reminders and habit tracking, turning your daily movements into productive triggers for communication and self-improvement.

📍

Precision Geofencing

Create places with customizable radii (50m+) and choose entry, exit, or both triggers for each location.

💬

WhatsApp Cloud API

Send template messages through Meta's official WhatsApp Business API — reliable, compliant, and secure.

🔔

Reminders & Habit Tracking

Set location-triggered reminders and track habits tied to places — like logging workouts when you reach the gym.

Features

Everything You Need for Location Automation

GeoWav combines geofencing, messaging, and productivity into a single, easy-to-use mobile experience.

📍

Location-Based Automation

Define geofenced places with custom radii and trigger types. GeoWav continuously monitors your location in the background and reacts when you cross a boundary.

💬

WhatsApp Alerts

Automatically send WhatsApp template messages to linked contacts when you enter or exit a geofenced location. Powered by Meta's official WhatsApp Business Cloud API.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Set reminders tied to specific locations. Get prompted to take action the moment you arrive somewhere — no more forgetting tasks when you reach a place.

📊

Habit Tracking

Track daily habits linked to the places you visit. Whether it's the gym, office, or library — GeoWav helps you build location-aware routines.

⚡

Real-Time Geofence Detection

High-accuracy GPS tracking with 20-meter distance intervals and 10-second update cycles. Detect boundary crossings reliably with Haversine-based distance calculations.

🔒

Built-In Message Deduplication

Intelligent two-layer deduplication prevents duplicate alerts — consecutive event filtering at the geofence level and a 5-minute cooldown per contact-place pair.

How It Works

Get Started in Five Simple Steps

Setting up location-based automation with GeoWav takes just a few minutes.

1

Create a Place

Search for a location using Google Places or long-press on the map to drop a pin. Set a geofence radius starting from 50 meters.

2

Add Your Contacts

Add the people you want to notify. Enter their name and phone number in E.164 format for WhatsApp delivery.

3

Configure Your Message

Write a custom message for each place, choose a trigger type (entry, exit, or both), and link the contacts who should receive it.

4

GeoWav Detects Your Location

The app continuously monitors your position in the background using high-accuracy GPS. When you cross a geofence boundary, an event is logged.

5

Messages Are Sent Automatically

Pending messages are queued and delivered via the WhatsApp Cloud API within seconds. You and your contacts stay informed — without lifting a finger.

Privacy & Data

Your Privacy Is Our Priority

GeoWav is designed with transparency and user control at its core. Here's how we handle your data.

🛡️

Location Data Stays Local

Your location data is processed on-device for geofence detection. It is used solely to determine whether you have entered or exited a defined place — and is never sold, shared, or transmitted to third-party analytics services.

🔐

No Data Monetization

GeoWav does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data, location history, or contact information to any third party. Your data exists to serve you — not advertisers.

✅

User-Defined Actions Only

Messages are sent only when you create a place, link a contact, and define a trigger. No messages are ever sent without your explicit configuration. You are always in control of what happens and when.

⚙️

Full User Control

You can create, edit, or delete places and contacts at any time. Removing a place immediately stops geofence monitoring and message triggers for that location. All automation is fully reversible.

WhatsApp API Compliance: GeoWav uses Meta's official WhatsApp Business Cloud API to send template messages. All messages are pre-approved templates that comply with Meta's messaging policies. Messages are delivered only to contacts that the user has explicitly added and linked to a geofenced place.
